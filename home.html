How SustainWear Works

1. Add your items

Use the donor dashboard to upload details like category, size, and condition for each item you want to donate.

2. We process donations

Your donations are reviewed, sorted, and prepared to be sent to charities, shelters, and community partners.

3. Clothes find new homes

Items are redistributed to people who need them most, extending their life and reducing waste.

Why Your Donations Matter

Reduce textile waste

Millions of tonnes of clothing end up in landfill every year. Donating helps keep good-quality items in use.

Support local communities

Your clothes can provide warmth, dignity, and confidence to people facing difficult circumstances.

Make sustainable choices

Small actions add up. Donating regularly is an easy way to live more sustainably.
